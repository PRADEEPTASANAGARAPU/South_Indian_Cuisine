Chilli Bajji

South Indian Spicy Fritter

Total Calories: 250 calories
Ingredients:
  • 6 green chilies (large, mild variety)
    		•
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons rice flour
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
    		•
  • Salt to taste
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    		•
  • Water as needed
    		•
  • Oil for deep frying
    		•
  • Chaat masala for garnish
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Preparing the chilies
Wash and slit the green chilies lengthwise without cutting through completely. Remove the seeds if you prefer less heat.

Step 2 - Preparing the batter
In a bowl, mix gram flour, rice flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin seeds, carom seeds, salt, and baking soda. Add water gradually to form a smooth, thick batter.

Step 3 - Coating the chilies
Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Dip each slit chili into the batter, ensuring it is well coated.

Step 4 - Frying
Carefully drop the coated chilies into hot oil. Fry until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain excess oil using a paper towel.

Step 5 - Serving
Sprinkle chaat masala on top and serve hot with chutney or ketchup.
