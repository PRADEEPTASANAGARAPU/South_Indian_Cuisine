Gutthi Vankaya Kura

Stuffed Brinjal Curry

Total Calories: 220 calories
Ingredients:
  • 8 small brinjals (eggplants) - Make deep slits while keeping the stem intact.
    		•
  • 2 onions, finely chopped - Forms the base of the stuffing.
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons roasted peanuts - Adds nutty flavor.
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds - Enhances the richness.
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut - Balances flavors.
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste - Provides depth to the dish.
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder - Adds color and antibacterial properties.
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder - Adjust spice level as needed.
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder - Enhances aroma.
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala - Brings authentic South Indian flavor.
    		•
  • Few curry leaves - For a signature taste.
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons oil - For frying.
    		•
  • Salt to taste - Enhances overall flavor.
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Preparing the stuffing
Grind roasted peanuts, sesame seeds, coconut, chopped onions, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt into a coarse paste.

Step 2 - Stuffing the brinjals
Gently stuff the prepared masala into the slits of the brinjals, ensuring they hold the mixture well.

Step 3 - Cooking the brinjals
Heat oil in a pan, add curry leaves and place the stuffed brinjals. Cover and cook on low flame for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 4 - Final touch
Sprinkle garam masala over the cooked brinjals, mix gently, and simmer for 5 more minutes.

Step 5 - Serving
Serve hot with steamed rice or roti for a delicious Andhra-style meal.
