Pootharekulu

Traditional Andhra Sweet

Total Calories: 200 calories per piece
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup rice flour - Used to make thin rice paper sheets.
    		•
  • 1 cup powdered jaggery or sugar - Adds sweetness.
    		•
  • ½ cup ghee - Enhances the flavor and helps in rolling.
    		•
  • ½ teaspoon cardamom powder - Adds a rich aroma.
    		•
  • Water as needed - To make the rice batter.
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Preparing rice sheets
Make a thin batter using rice flour and water. Heat a pan, grease it lightly, and pour a very thin layer of batter. Let it cook for a few seconds, then remove carefully.

Step 2 - Preparing the filling
Mix powdered jaggery (or sugar) with cardamom powder. Melt ghee and keep it aside.

Step 3 - Assembling Pootharekulu
Place a rice paper sheet on a clean surface, apply a thin layer of ghee, sprinkle the sugar-jaggery mixture, and carefully roll or fold it into layers.

Step 4 - Final Touch
Repeat the process for multiple sheets and layer them to get crispy, delicious Pootharekulu.

Step 5 - Serving
Serve fresh or store in an airtight container for later enjoyment.
