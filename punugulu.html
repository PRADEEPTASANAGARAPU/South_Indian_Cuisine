Method:
Step 1- Preparing the batter
In a mixing bowl, take dosa batter. Add rice flour, chopped onions, green chilies, cumin seeds, chopped ginger, curry leaves, and salt. Mix well.

Step 2- Heating oil
Heat oil in a deep pan on medium flame. Once the oil is hot, take small portions of the batter and drop them into the oil.

Step 3- Frying Punugulu
Fry them in batches until they turn golden brown and crispy. Stir occasionally to ensure even frying. Remove and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

Step 4- Serving
Serve hot with coconut chutney or peanut chutney for a delicious snack.
